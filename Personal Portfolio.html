Logo Design

My logo is a monogram of my initials with eyes, creating a face-like effect. This design choice reflects my eye for detail and user-centered approach in UX. The eyes symbolize observation and insight, which are crucial to my visual design philosophy.

Above: Final Logo Design for Portofolio Site.

XII

Prototype

I then infused my style guide into the mockup to create a high-fidelity prototype, ensuring that my portfolio's design was consistent, visually appealing, and aligned with my personal brand.

XIII

Wireframing

Upon completing a stylised prototype, I transitioned to wireframing my portfolio design, laying the foundational structure and functionality to ensure a seamless and intuitive user experience.

XIV

HTML & CSS Build

After completing the wireframe, I began the development phase by constructing my website in VS Code, crafting each webpage using HTML, CSS & Javascript to define its layout, functionality and stylistic elements.

XII

Hosting

Once I completed the development of my portfolio website, I proceeded to launch it on the internet by hosting it on GitHub, enabling widespread accessibility and showcasing my work to a global audience and potential employers.
