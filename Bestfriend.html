Pet Matchmaking App

Overview

"BestFriend" is a groundbreaking match-making app designed to facilitate the adoption process for rescue dogs. Users create personalized profiles and complete surveys to identify their ideal furry companion. With a user-friendly swipe interface, individuals can browse through potential matches and easily connect with rescue shelters. BestFriend not only simplifies the adoption journey but also fosters meaningful connections between humans and their future four-legged best friends.
