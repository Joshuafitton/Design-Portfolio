CRM Design

Overview

"BestFriend" is a groundbreaking match-making app designed to facilitate the adoption process for rescue dogs. Users create personalized profiles and complete surveys to identify their ideal furry companion. With a user-friendly swipe interface, individuals can browse through potential matches and easily connect with rescue shelters. BestFriend not only simplifies the adoption journey but also fosters meaningful connections between humans and their future four-legged best friends.

II

| Competitor Research |

Brand & Product

Microsoft

Strengths

  • Offers extensive customization capabilities.
  • Provides a consistent and intuitive user interface.
  • Robust analytics and reporting features.

Weaknesses

  • Extensive features and capabilities may require significant training.
  • Complexity in configuration and setup..
  • Integration with third-party applications and services may be limited.

SalesForce

Strengths

  • Intuitive user interface with customizable layout.
  • Offers a vast ecosystem of third-party integrations.
  • Allows users to access CRM data via mobile.

Weaknesses

  • Complexity in configuration
  • Heavy reliance on third-party apps may lead to compatibility issues.
  • Offline capabilities are limited compared to other platforms

Oracle

Strengths

  • Comprehensive suite of applications.
  • Designed to scale with the needs of growing businesses.
  • eamlessly integrates with other Oracle products and services.

Weaknesses

  • Complexity in implementation
  • limitations in terms of flexibility and ease of customization.
  • Users may face a steep learning curve when adopting the CRM.
